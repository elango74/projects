About Greece

Greece, officially the Hellenic Republic, is a country in Southeast Europe. Known for its ancient history, stunning islands, and Mediterranean cuisine, Greece is a dream destination for travelers worldwide.

As the cradle of Western civilization, Greece is the birthplace of democracy, philosophy, the Olympic Games, and much more. Its rich cultural heritage is visible in its ancient ruins, museums, and traditional villages.
